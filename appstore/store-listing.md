## Full Description

Fit Check is the app for people who care about what they wear — and want honest feedback before they walk out the door.

Your mirror can't tell you if the fit actually works. Your friends are too polite. Your Instagram followers are too busy. Fit Check connects you with a community of style-forward people who give you real, fast, anonymous ratings on your outfits — so you know what's working and what's not before it matters.

Post a fit, get rated:
- Snap or upload an outfit photo
- Add optional tags (occasion, aesthetic, budget range)
- The community rates it across style, coordination, and occasion-appropriateness
- See your ratings in real time as votes come in
- Read comments from people who know what they're talking about

Rate other fits:
- Swipe through a feed of community fits
- Give a quick rating (1-10) or a detailed breakdown
- Leave constructive feedback or a compliment
- Build your reputation as a trusted voice in the community

Fit styles and aesthetics:
- Streetwear
- Business casual
- Date night
- Festival / concert
- Athleisure
- Old money / quiet luxury
- Y2K and vintage
- Coastal grandmother (yes, really)
- Dark academia
- Your own custom tags

Wardrobe builder:
- Log your outfits over time
- See your highest-rated looks
- Track what pieces get the most compliments
- Build a visual record of what actually works for you

Shop the look:
Tap any clothing item in a rated outfit to find similar pieces. Fit Check connects to major retailers so when you see a look you love, you can find something like it without the hours of searching.

Anonymous by default:
You don't have to show your face. Post outfit photos cropped to the clothing, get ratings on the fit itself — not your appearance. Fit Check is about the clothes, not the body.

Community standards:
Fit Check has a zero-tolerance policy for negative body commentary. Ratings are about style, coordination, and occasion fit — full stop. The moderation team removes anything that crosses that line.

Whether you're building your style, prepping for a specific event, or just want validation that yes, that jacket does go with those pants — Fit Check gives you the community feedback loop that everyone wishes they had.

Download Fit Check. Get the feedback your mirror can't give you.
